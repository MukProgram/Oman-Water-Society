The Oman Water Society participated in organizing the Oman Facilities and Environment Conference and Exhibition, which was held during the period (5-7) October 2020. Its theme is “Sustainable facilities for a healthy environment.”

The Oman Water Society will participate in the scientific committee of the conference, as the sustainable future of water is a global concern and is of particular importance when water resources are scarce. A sustainable water future can only be achieved through responsible management

The Oman Water Society (OWS), in partnership with the Water Producers Association in the Middle East and Petroleum Development Oman (PDO), organized a conference on water associated with oil during the period on the 23rd and 24th of October at the Sheraton Oman Hotel in Muscat.

The project aims to compose a book on water resources in the Sultanate of Oman, record them and issue a guide that includes all sources, their locations, names, history and related works. The project comes after several books, pamphlets and separate scientific publications on the water situation in the Sultanate, which provided readers and interested people with information about water sources and types. His Highness the Honorary President of the Society would like to submit the project to the Diwan of Royal Court for approval.

On the initiative of His Highness Sayyid Taimur bin Asaad bin Tariq Al Said, the honorary president of the association, and in order to exchange ideas and experiences and unify efforts, the Omani Water Association seeks to organize a meeting every two years for the associations operating in the Sultanate, in which the efforts made to achieve the goals of these associations are presented. One of the most important objectives of the forum, in addition to highlighting the efforts made by the associations, is to promote a culture of volunteer work.
