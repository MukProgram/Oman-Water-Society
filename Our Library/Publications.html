The Oman Water Society participated in organizing the Oman Facilities and Environment Conference and Exhibition, which was held during the period (5-7) October 2020. Its theme is “Sustainable facilities for a healthy environment.”

The Oman Water Society will participate in the scientific committee of the conference, as the sustainable future of water is a global concern and is of particular importance when water resources are scarce. A sustainable water future can only be achieved through responsible management

The Oman Water Society (OWS), in partnership with the Water Producers Association in the Middle East and Petroleum Development Oman (PDO), organized a conference on water associated with oil during the period on the 23rd and 24th of October at the Sheraton Oman Hotel in Muscat.
